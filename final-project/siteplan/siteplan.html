Bountiful Foods

Purpose

Bountiful Foods has a physical presence like their storefront and food trucks, but what they're missing is an online presence to get the attention of potential customers living in California that are not aware of their presence and also keep reminded their customers of their presence. The purpose is to establish an online presence showing potential customers what they have to offer: fresh and healthy food options, including organic, made to order fruit drinks and fruit bags.


Logo


Audience

Bountiful Foods target audience are the local population and the tourists visiting the coastal areas of California. These people like to eat fresh, healthy food and are more likely to spend extra if than mean that they're getting organic food. They care a lot about what they eat and do their best to find the healthiest food possible in the area, they like to live their healthy lifestyle while supporting local businesses and farmers.


Personas

Jennifer is 36 years old, single, works in tech and like to treat herself. She is very conscious about what she eats and wants to eat fresh and healthy food. She will drive a long distance if that means that she can buy healthy food. She exercises on a regular basis and likes a good drink. She likes to support small businesses and local farms by buying locally.

Thomas is 26 years old and lived in California his whole life. He's a gym rat and watch closely the type of food he eats. He likes to eat healthy food and only buys organic food from locals. He likes to know where his food come from and only trust local farmers where he's certain to get fresh and healthy food.

Annika is 24 years old, single and has been trying to lose weight for some now. She's now trying to change her bad habit of eating too much processed food and has been trying to eat healthier. She now consume way more fruit than ever and likes to buy fresh and healthy food locally.


Scenarios

First Scenario: The user is trying to eat healthier in order to lose weight and after a few search on his personal computer to find options ended up on Bountiful Foods website.

Second Scenario: The user heard about the Bountiful Foods from his coworker and the curiosity led him/her to the website to find out more about the business and what they stand for.

Third Scenario: The user loves fruit drinks and after a few search to find a new spot to try fruit drinks ended up on Bountiful Foods website.
