About Us

Carlsbad

Carlsbad is a coastal city in the North County region of San Diego County, California, United States. The city is 87 miles (140 km) south of downtown Los Angeles and 35 miles (56 km) north of downtown San Diego. As of the 2020 census, the population of the city was 114,746. Carlsbad is a popular tourist destination and home to many businesses in the golf industry.

A little bit of History

Bountiful Foods has been created in 2000 after the founder Becky Ma realized that there was a need for healthy fruit drink in the coastal area were people like to drink a good fruit drink when it is hot. The company started with one food truck, after 5 years of business decided it was time to open our first store in Carlsbad. The company haven't stop expanding since then, the business went from 1 food truck to 50 food trucks located in California to have a better reach and service customers in California.
