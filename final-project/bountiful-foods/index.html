Santa Barbara

The city lies between the steeply rising Santa Ynez and the Pacific Ocean with a climate usually described as Mediterranean. This beautiful coastal city is known for its broad and sandy beaches. Whether you're looking to catch the perfect wave or the perfect sunset, they're plenty to choose from.
