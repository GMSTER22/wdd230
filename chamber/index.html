News Release

As you may already know, the chamber is celebrating 75 years of helping professionals, entrepreneurs, and business owners build great relationships. The chamber has been engaged to make this city a better place for all its inhabitants, putting everything in place to attract investors, the goal is to make this city a great place for business. The future, we want to design it with you, a big surprise is coming soon for our most loyal members.
