TARGET AUDIENCE

Audience

Our target audience are business owners, entrepreneurs, professionals looking to connect and network for the success of their businesses. These people are looking for ways to grow their businesses, they're passionate about what they do and want to network with people like them for potential collaboration or opportunities that they would not find elsewhere. A lot of businesses can happen when a lot of businessmen occupy the same room or work together and the Chamber of Commerce is a great opportunity for those living in Ouagadougou to make things happen.

Personas

Jonathan Okka

Jonathan is 45 years old, married and father of 5 children, he is a High School dropout. He is a trader, he has been in the trade business for over 20 years. He owns 2 businesses that imports goods from Europe and sell them in Ouagadougou, Burkina Faso. He is looking to expand his activities, be more diverse, and is now exploring his possibilities and potential collaboration. He is not tech savvy but is smart enough to hire employees who are and can give him an edge. He has great social skills and like to network.

Vivien Sanogo

Vivien is 24 years old, he is single, childless and has a Master's in Electrical Engineering. He is a young entrepreneur with a lot of great ideas, his last 2 businesses failed and he is looking to start his third one. He specialized in building useful web applications and also in the renewable energy business. He is tech savvy and determined to succeed in his next venture. He's looking to learn from others, grow his network and collaborate to bring his ideas to life.

Scenarios

First Scenario: The user heard about the Chamber of Commerce and reached the website from his home computer trying to figure out what the Chamber could do for him.

Second Scenario: The user knows about the Chamber of Commerce and like networking with other business owners, so he constantly reached the home page of the website using his phone to keep up to date with the different events that the Chamber of Commerce organize.

Third Scenario: The user is trying to be part of the Chamber of Commerce. He connects from his work computer to gather some information about how he could join the Chamber of Commerce, he wants to know if there are fees associated with it and the different benefits and privileges that each membership cards would get him if they exists.

Fourth Scenario: The user is curious about the Chamber of Commerce and wants to see the impact of the Chamber of Commerce on its participants, the community and how effective it is to network with other professionals. H accessed the website through his home computer.

Fifth Scenario: The user is a young entrepreneur who uses his phone to visit the website in order to figure out if the Chamber of Commerce is providing some type of help or has some programs to gives some visibility to young entrepreneur.
