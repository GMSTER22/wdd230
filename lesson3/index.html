OUAGADOUGOU CHAMBER OF COMMERCE

Purpose

We need a website because nowadays an online presence is really critical to the success of any organization or business. The purpose of the Ouagadougou Chamber of Commerce website is to reach professionals, business owners, anyone in the city of Ouagadougou interested in connecting, networking, and growing their businesses with the help of others. The website is a way to highlight the many ways the Ouagadougou Chamber of Commerce can help in the promotion of businesses, their actions to promote them, and advertise the attractions of the city.
